Our Strategy

  • Deliver targeted wells safely, efficiently and cost effectively as per business plan.

  • Maintain knowledge sharing and transfer knowledge to all EDD employees.

  • Commit to the kingdom environmental vision and protection policy’s including GHG to minimize impacts to the environment, and protect natural resource

  • Adhere to the rules, regulations and comply with the requirements of HSE programs throughout our drilling operations.

  • Promote innovation and implementation of innovative idea and recognize employees.

  • Promote OE culture in all of EDD day-to-day activities.
